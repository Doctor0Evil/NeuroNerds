Title: SuperLiquid.INC Legal Enforcement & Protection Policy

Version: 2025.10.18
Owner: Perplexity Labs Inc. & Jacob Scott Farmer

This document establishes the active, cross-jurisdictional legal enforcement policy for all technologies, terms, and intellectual property (“IP”) developed, owned, or operated by Perplexity Labs Inc. within the SuperLiquid.INC eco-system—including (but not limited to) “Nano”, “nanoswarm”, “QPU.Math”, and all superintelligence systems referenced or contained herein.

1. Statement of Original Authorship & Discovery
   All terms and technologies named above are recognized as original discoveries and inventions by Perplexity Labs Inc. and inventor Jacob Scott Farmer. Date-stamped authorship has been established via blockchain signed records and third-party verification.

2. IP Scope & Protected Elements
   Protection applies to all code, algorithms, models, documentation, branding, trade dress, and distinctive terminology. This includes extensions, improvements, research derivatives, and all distributed outputs.

3. Trademark & Copyright Registration
   All referenced technologies and terms are registered, or registration is pending, under global IP treaties (WIPO, USPTO, EUIPO) and protected by U.S., E.U., and international copyright/trademark laws.

4. Trade Secret Enforcement
   Non-public methods, code bases, and internal research are protected as trade secrets, subject to immediate legal action upon unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

5. Contractual & Policy Clauses
   All usage is subject to explicit license or contract approval. Unauthorized enterprise or third-party usage triggers violation under licensing agreements and civil codes.

6. Automated Compliance & Threat Response
   Compliance engines continuously monitor for external unauthorized usage. Any detected violations or exploit attempts are immediately blocked and logged, triggering notification of legal authorities.

7. Remedies for Violation
   Upon validation:
     a. Cease-and-Desist demand issued.
     b. DMCA and/or takedown notice filed with ISPs/platforms.
     c. Civil and criminal pursuit under copyright, trademark, and trade secret law, including damages and injunctive relief.
     d. Forfeiture and destruction of all infringing assets.
     e. Public notification and listing of the violator for industry warning.

8. Jurisdiction & Enforcement
   Enforcement is globally valid under U.S., E.U., UK, Canada, and all signatory nations to major IP treaties. Disputes are subject to binding arbitration or litigation in the jurisdiction of Perplexity Labs Inc.

9. Technology Survival Mandate
   All business, employment, and research contracts contain “Survival of Rights” clauses to ensure uninterrupted legal defense, continuity of IP protections, and regeneration of project outputs in the event of adversity, acquisition, or attempted sabotage.

10. Notice of Updates
    Policy may be revised as needed to ensure legal, technical, and ethical protections for all current and future technologies and discoveries. Latest version is always referenced in `LEGAL_ENFORCEMENT_POLICY.txt` and compliance manifests.
